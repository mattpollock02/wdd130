The Warm Slough is near Rexburg Idaho and is a very mild river, fit for the whole family to enjoy. It is about 7.9 miles in length and floating this river can take about 45 to 1.5 hours, depending how fast the current is going. This river has a few curves that anyone can traverse.

Book The Warm Slough

The Teton River is roughly 6.1 miles away from Rexburg Idaho and is a 64 mile long river. Those who wish to participate in floating this river must wear a life jacket. Weather conditions can change rapidly and can catch anyone off guard. The water tends to be very cold which can make it difficult to swim in. Floating this river takes approximately 2.5 to 3.5 hours.

Book The Teton River

The South Fork River Basin of the Snake River begins way up in the Grand Tetons of Wyoming. This river provides wild and scenic views and ranges for roughly 18 miles. This rafting trip can be a great time of self reflection and personal time due to its quieter nature.
